My Work

Fund Forward

Fund Forward was the winning concept made for the 2024 uXperience Design Jam. The goal was to find a solution for the lack of financial literacy for people ages 24-34. My team spent a day creating Fund Forward, an educational website that allows you to visualize your financial future. We visualize the users financial goals using an interactive graph that highlights life milestones and their financial impact.
